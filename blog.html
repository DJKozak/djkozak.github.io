About

This is a blog about visualizing genealogy data, with the lofty goal of displaying a single diagram that shows how a single person (like you), is related to everyone that has ever been born. Specifically, I describe algorithms I created as part of my final project to complete my Masters Degree in Computer Science at the University of Minnesota. But the blog is formatted as a narative and I also discuss my motivations and related topics.
