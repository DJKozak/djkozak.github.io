    About this page:

    The general structure of this page was originally based on a timline example by Sergio Mandrake, but there are many very similar examples online, so it's not clear if he was the originator or not. This example by "luisrudge" was helpful as well, and for all I know may have been the original as well.

    The record label images are from Discogs and Sun Records Discography. The image of Johnny Cash himself comes from Wikipedia.

    The map was added using the instructions found on google.
